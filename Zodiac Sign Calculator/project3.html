Zodiac Sign Calculator

Back to Main Page

Zodiac Sign Calculator is a simple Android application that determines a user's zodiac sign based on their birthdate. Users can input their birthday, and with a single tap, the app calculates and displays their corresponding zodiac sign.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly interface for inputting birth dates
  • Accurate calculation of zodiac signs based on birth dates
  • Instant display of the user's zodiac sign
  • Simple and intuitive design for ease of use

Technologies Used:

  • Java
  • Android Studio
  • Android SDK

This Zodiac Sign Calculator app serves as an excellent example of several fundamental concepts in Android app development.
