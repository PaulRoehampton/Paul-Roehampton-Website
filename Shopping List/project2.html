Shopping List

Back to Main Page

The Shopping List App is a streamlined, user-friendly mobile application designed to simplify your shopping experience. This intuitive app allows users to create, manage, and organize their shopping lists effortlessly. Whether you're planning a quick grocery run or a major shopping spree, this app keeps you organized and ensures you never forget an item again. The app addresses common shopping challenges by providing a digital solution to paper lists that are easily lost or forgotten.

Key Features:

  • Dual List Management: Maintain separate lists for shopping items and purchased items.
  • Real-Time Updates: Easily move items between shopping and purchased lists with a simple tap.
  • Quick Add Functionality: Rapidly add new items to your shopping list using the custom add button.
  • Item Deletion: Remove items from either list with a dedicated delete button for each entry.
  • Persistent Storage: Lists are automatically saved and retrieved between app sessions, ensuring your data is never lost.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean, intuitive design for effortless navigation and use.
  • Offline Functionality: Full app functionality without requiring an internet connection.

Technologies Used:

  • Kotlin
  • Android SDK
  • RecyclerView
  • SharedPreferences
  • Gson
  • Material Design
  • Custom Views

This app demonstrates proficiency in Android app development, showcasing skills in UI/UX design, data persistence, and efficient list management in a mobile environment.
